Heart Conditions We Treat

Comprehensive care for a wide range of cardiovascular conditions

Coronary Artery Disease

Treatment for blocked or narrowed coronary arteries that can lead to chest pain, heart attacks, or other serious complications.

Heart Failure

Comprehensive management of heart failure, including advanced therapies and lifestyle interventions to improve quality of life.

Arrhythmias

Diagnosis and treatment of irregular heart rhythms, including atrial fibrillation, ventricular tachycardia, and bradycardia.

Hypertension

Specialized care for high blood pressure, including medication management and lifestyle modification programs.

Valvular Heart Disease

Treatment for conditions affecting heart valves, including stenosis, regurgitation, and prolapse.

Peripheral Artery Disease

Management of circulatory problems caused by narrowed arteries reducing blood flow to limbs.

Advanced Cardiac Diagnostics

State-of-the-art diagnostic technology for accurate heart assessment

Echocardiography

Advanced ultrasound imaging of the heart to assess structure and function, including 3D echo and stress echo.

Cardiac MRI

High-resolution imaging to evaluate heart structure, function, and tissue characteristics without radiation.

Electrophysiology Studies

Specialized tests to evaluate the heart's electrical system and diagnose complex arrhythmias.

Stress Testing

Exercise and pharmacological stress tests to evaluate heart function under controlled conditions.

Cardiac Catheterization

Minimally invasive procedure to diagnose and treat coronary artery disease and structural heart conditions.

Holter & Event Monitoring

Extended heart rhythm monitoring to detect intermittent arrhythmias and assess treatment effectiveness.

Cardiac Treatments & Procedures

Comprehensive interventional and surgical treatments for heart conditions

Coronary Angioplasty & Stenting

Minimally invasive procedure to open blocked coronary arteries and restore blood flow to the heart muscle.

  • Balloon angioplasty
  • Drug-eluting stents
  • Fractional flow reserve
  • Optical coherence tomography

Cardiac Bypass Surgery

Open-heart surgery to create new routes for blood to flow around blocked coronary arteries.

  • Coronary artery bypass grafting
  • Off-pump CABG
  • Minimally invasive approaches
  • Arterial and venous grafts

Heart Valve Surgery

Surgical repair or replacement of diseased heart valves to restore proper blood flow.

  • Valve repair procedures
  • Mechanical & biological valves
  • Transcatheter aortic valve replacement
  • Mitral valve clip procedures

Arrhythmia Treatment

Advanced therapies for heart rhythm disorders including catheter ablation and device implantation.

  • Catheter ablation
  • Pacemaker implantation
  • Implantable defibrillators
  • Cardiac resynchronization therapy

Heart Failure Management

Comprehensive care for heart failure including advanced medical therapy and device-based treatments.

  • Ventricular assist devices
  • Cardiac rehabilitation
  • Advanced medication regimens
  • Heart transplant evaluation

Preventive Cardiology

Comprehensive risk assessment and management to prevent heart disease and promote cardiovascular health.

  • Cardiovascular risk assessment
  • Lipid management
  • Lifestyle modification programs
  • Genetic counseling for heart disease

Cardiac Prevention & Rehabilitation

Comprehensive programs to prevent heart disease and support recovery

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Supervised exercise and education program to help patients recover after heart procedures and improve cardiovascular health.

Nutrition Counseling

Personalized dietary plans to manage cholesterol, blood pressure, and weight for optimal heart health.

Exercise Physiology

Customized exercise programs designed to improve cardiovascular fitness and reduce heart disease risk factors.

Smoking Cessation

Comprehensive programs to help patients quit smoking and reduce cardiovascular risk.

Stress Management

Techniques and therapies to manage stress, which is a significant risk factor for heart disease.

Support Groups

Peer support and education for patients and families dealing with heart conditions.

Our Multidisciplinary Approach

At BN Hospital Cardiology, we believe in a team-based approach to heart care, bringing together specialists from various fields to develop comprehensive treatment plans.

  • Cardiologists & Cardiac Surgeons

    Our team includes interventional cardiologists, electrophysiologists, and cardiac surgeons with specialized expertise.

  • Cardiac Nurses & Technologists

    Highly skilled nursing staff and cardiac technologists provide exceptional patient care and support.

  • Cardiac Rehabilitation Specialists

    Exercise physiologists, dietitians, and therapists help patients recover and maintain heart health.

  • Continuous Care Coordination

    From diagnosis through recovery and prevention, we provide seamless coordination of care.
