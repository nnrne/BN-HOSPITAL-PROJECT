Advanced Neurological Diagnostics

State-of-the-art diagnostic technology for accurate neurological assessment

MRI & fMRI

High-resolution brain and spine imaging including functional MRI for brain activity mapping.

CT Scanning

Advanced computed tomography for rapid assessment of strokes, hemorrhages, and traumatic injuries.

EEG & Video Monitoring

Comprehensive electroencephalography for seizure detection and brain function assessment.

Lumbar Puncture

Cerebrospinal fluid analysis for diagnosis of infections, inflammation, and neurological disorders.

Nerve Conduction Studies

Electromyography and nerve conduction velocity testing for peripheral nerve disorders.

Neuro-ophthalmology

Comprehensive visual system evaluation for neurological conditions affecting vision.

Our Multidisciplinary Approach

At BN Hospital Neurology, we believe in a team-based approach to neurological care, bringing together specialists from various fields to develop comprehensive treatment plans.

  • Neurologists & Neurosurgeons

    Our team includes specialists in stroke, epilepsy, movement disorders, neurosurgery, and neurocritical care.

  • Neurodiagnostic Specialists

    Highly skilled technicians and radiologists provide advanced imaging and diagnostic testing.

  • Rehabilitation Team

    Physical, occupational, and speech therapists work together to maximize recovery and functional outcomes.

  • Comprehensive Care Coordination

    From diagnosis through recovery and long-term management, we provide seamless coordination of care.
