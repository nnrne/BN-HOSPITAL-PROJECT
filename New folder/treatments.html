Our Medical Specialties

Comprehensive healthcare services across all major medical specialties

Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery

Comprehensive heart care including diagnostic tests, interventional procedures, and cardiac surgery.

  • Coronary angioplasty and stenting
  • Cardiac bypass surgery
  • Heart valve repair and replacement
  • Arrhythmia treatment and pacemakers

Orthopedics & Joint Replacement

Advanced treatments for bone, joint, and muscle conditions including minimally invasive surgeries.

  • Hip and knee replacement
  • Arthroscopic surgery
  • Sports injury treatment
  • Spinal surgery and disc replacement

Oncology & Cancer Care

Multidisciplinary cancer treatment including chemotherapy, radiation, and surgical oncology.

  • Medical oncology and chemotherapy
  • Radiation therapy
  • Surgical oncology
  • Immunotherapy and targeted therapy

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Comprehensive care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders using advanced technology.

  • Brain tumor surgery
  • Stroke treatment and rehabilitation
  • Spinal surgery and disc treatment
  • Epilepsy and movement disorder management

Gastroenterology & Endoscopy

Diagnosis and treatment of digestive system disorders using advanced endoscopic techniques.

  • Colonoscopy and gastroscopy
  • Endoscopic ultrasound
  • Inflammatory bowel disease treatment
  • Liver disease management

Urology & Men's Health

Comprehensive urological care for kidney, bladder, prostate, and male reproductive health.

  • Prostate cancer treatment
  • Kidney stone treatment
  • Bladder surgery
  • Male fertility treatments

Our Patient-Centered Approach

At BN Hospital, we believe in treating the whole person, not just the condition. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures comprehensive care tailored to your unique needs.

  • Multidisciplinary Teams

    Specialists from different fields collaborate to develop comprehensive treatment plans.

  • Personalized Treatment Plans

    Every patient receives a customized treatment approach based on their specific condition and preferences.

  • Holistic Care

    We address physical, emotional, and psychological aspects of health for complete wellbeing.

  • Continuous Support

    From diagnosis through recovery, our team provides ongoing support and guidance.
