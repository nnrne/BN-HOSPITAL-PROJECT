Cancer Types We Treat

Comprehensive care for a wide range of cancer types with specialized treatment approaches

Breast Cancer

Comprehensive care including surgery, chemotherapy, radiation, and targeted therapies for all stages of breast cancer.

Lung Cancer

Advanced treatments for lung cancer including minimally invasive surgery, immunotherapy, and precision radiation.

Prostate Cancer

Specialized care including active surveillance, robotic surgery, and advanced radiation techniques.

Brain Tumors

Multidisciplinary approach to brain cancer including neurosurgery, radiation oncology, and neuro-oncology.

Blood Cancers

Comprehensive hematologic oncology including leukemia, lymphoma, and multiple myeloma treatments.

Gastrointestinal Cancers

Specialized care for colorectal, pancreatic, liver, and other digestive system cancers.

Advanced Cancer Diagnostics

State-of-the-art diagnostic technology for accurate cancer detection and staging

PET-CT Scanning

Advanced imaging that combines PET and CT to detect cancer, determine staging, and monitor treatment response.

Pathology & Biopsy

Comprehensive tissue analysis including immunohistochemistry and molecular profiling for precise diagnosis.

Genetic Testing

Advanced genomic testing to identify specific mutations and guide targeted therapy selection.

Liquid Biopsy

Minimally invasive blood tests to detect cancer DNA and monitor treatment response over time.

Radiation Simulation

Precise planning and simulation for radiation therapy treatments using advanced imaging technology.

Cancer Screening

Comprehensive screening programs for early detection of breast, colon, lung, and prostate cancers.

Our Multidisciplinary Approach

At BN Hospital Oncology, we believe in a team-based approach to cancer care, bringing together specialists from various fields to develop comprehensive treatment plans.

  • Medical, Radiation & Surgical Oncologists

    Our team includes specialists in all cancer treatment modalities working together to create optimal treatment plans.

  • Oncology Nurses & Navigators

    Highly skilled nursing staff and patient navigators provide exceptional care coordination and support.

  • Pathologists & Genetic Counselors

    Expert diagnostic and genetic professionals ensure accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment approaches.

  • Supportive Care Team

    From diagnosis through survivorship, we provide comprehensive support for physical and emotional needs.
