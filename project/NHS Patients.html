NHS Care at BN Hospital London

BN Hospital London proudly supports NHS patients by delivering safe, effective and compassionate care within NHS pathways.

We work in collaboration with NHS commissioners and trusts to ensure patients receive access to specialist services, reduced waiting times and excellent clinical outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do NHS patients pay for treatment?
No. All approved NHS treatments are fully funded.

Can I choose BN Hospital London?
Yes, patients may be offered BN Hospital London as part of NHS choice.

Is care different from private patients?
Clinical quality and safety standards are the same for all patients.
