General Surgery

General Surgery Department

Advanced surgical care delivered with safety, precision and compassion.

BN Hospital London provides consultant-led General Surgery services covering the diagnosis and operative management of abdominal, gastrointestinal and soft tissue conditions. Our department combines modern surgical techniques with holistic patient-centred care.

Patient Journey

A clear and structured pathway from referral to recovery

Initial Consultation

Comprehensive assessment with a consultant surgeon including medical history, examination and investigations.

Diagnostic Tests

Imaging, laboratory tests or endoscopic investigations are arranged where clinically indicated.

Surgical Treatment

Elective or emergency surgery is carried out using evidence-based techniques in modern operating theatres.

Post-Operative Care

Dedicated recovery ward care, pain control and early mobilisation led by specialist nursing teams.

Follow-Up & Recovery

Outpatient reviews, wound care, rehabilitation advice and long-term outcome monitoring.

Clinical Environment & Facilities

Operating Theatres: Fully equipped with advanced laparoscopic systems.

Recovery Suites: Monitored post-operative environments.

Diagnostic Imaging: Rapid access to CT, MRI and ultrasound imaging.

Ward Facilities: Comfortable inpatient accommodation.

Clinical Governance & Patient Safety

Patient safety is central to all surgical care delivered at BN Hospital. Our General Surgery department adheres to national and international healthcare standards.

  • Compliance with NICE and NHS clinical guidelines
  • Consultants registered with GMC and relevant Royal Colleges
  • World Health Organization (WHO) surgical safety checklist
  • Strict infection prevention and control policies
  • Regular clinical audits and outcome monitoring
