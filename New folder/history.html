Our History & Legacy

Discover the journey of BN Hospital London from a small Harley Street practice to a premier private healthcare institution

Our Historical Journey

Over three decades of medical excellence, innovation, and compassionate care

1985

The Foundation

BN Hospital London was founded by Dr. Benjamin Northwood as a small private practice on Harley Street. With just three consulting rooms and a vision for exceptional patient care, the foundation was laid for what would become one of London's most respected private hospitals.

1992

First Expansion

After seven years of successful operation, BN Hospital expanded to acquire the adjacent property, doubling its capacity. This expansion included the addition of the first dedicated surgical suite and the establishment of our renowned cardiology department.

1998

Teaching Hospital Status

BN Hospital was officially recognized as a teaching hospital, establishing partnerships with leading medical schools. This marked the beginning of our commitment to medical education and the training of future healthcare professionals.

2005

Major Renovation & Modernization

A comprehensive Â£15 million renovation project transformed BN Hospital into a state-of-the-art medical facility. The project included the installation of advanced diagnostic equipment, modern patient rooms, and the creation of our acclaimed cancer treatment center.

2012

International Recognition

BN Hospital received the International Healthcare Accreditation, recognizing our commitment to the highest standards of patient care and safety. This year also marked the opening of our specialized international patient services department.

2020

Pandemic Response & Innovation

During the global pandemic, BN Hospital established one of London's first dedicated private COVID-19 testing and treatment centers. We also launched our telemedicine service, allowing patients to consult with specialists remotely.

2023

Present Day Excellence

Today, BN Hospital stands as one of London's premier private healthcare institutions, with over 200 medical professionals, state-of-the-art facilities, and an unwavering commitment to patient-centered care. Our legacy continues to grow as we embrace new medical technologies and treatments.
