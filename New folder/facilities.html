Medical Facilities & Services

Comprehensive healthcare infrastructure designed for excellence in patient care

24/7 Emergency Department

Our fully-equipped emergency department is staffed round the clock by experienced emergency medicine specialists, ready to handle any medical crisis.

  • Trauma center with resuscitation bays
  • Dedicated pediatric emergency area
  • Rapid diagnostic imaging services
  • Direct admission to critical care units

Advanced Operating Theaters

State-of-the-art surgical suites equipped with the latest technology for minimally invasive and complex surgical procedures across all specialties.

  • Laminar airflow systems
  • Integrated robotic surgery systems
  • Advanced anesthesia monitoring
  • Hybrid operating rooms

Comprehensive Diagnostic Imaging

Advanced radiology department featuring cutting-edge imaging technology for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

  • 3T MRI and CT scanners
  • Digital mammography with tomosynthesis
  • PET-CT and nuclear medicine
  • Interventional radiology suite

Critical Care Units

Specialized intensive care units providing advanced life support and monitoring for critically ill patients.

  • Medical and surgical ICUs
  • Cardiac care unit with telemetry
  • Neonatal and pediatric ICUs
  • High-dependency step-down units

Family-Centered Maternity Care

Comprehensive obstetric services in a comfortable, home-like environment designed for the entire family.

  • Labor-delivery-recovery suites
  • Level III neonatal intensive care
  • Water birth facilities
  • Lactation consultation services

Comprehensive Rehabilitation Center

Multidisciplinary rehabilitation services to help patients regain function and independence after illness or injury.

  • Physical and occupational therapy
  • Speech and language pathology
  • Hydrotherapy pool
  • Gymnasium with specialized equipment
